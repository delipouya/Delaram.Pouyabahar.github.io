Research Experience

University of Toronto | Jan 2020 – Present

Terrence Donnelly Centre - Supervisor: Dr. Gary Bader

  • Using statistical and machine learning approaches, developed a computational framework to identify interpretable signals in multifactorial single-cell maps.
  • Constructed the first rat single-cell liver atlas and performed inter-strain comparison in a rat liver transplant model in collaboration with the MacParland Lab.
  • Compared single-cell transcriptomics maps of tolerant and rejected rat livers to discover therapeutic targets for modulating the liver microenvironment toward graft acceptance.
  • Generated a single-cell transcriptomics map of hPSCs-derived human cardiac cells to guide in vitro sinoatrial node differentiation in collaboration with the Protze lab, UHN, Toronto. Manuscript in review.

Sharif University of Technology | July 2018 – Aug 2019

Bioinformatics Research Group - Supervisor: Dr. Ali Sharifi-Zarchi

  • Investigated the genomic principles of small RNA sorting into exosomes using machine learning and deep learning methods in collaboration with the Goodarzi Lab, University of California, San Francisco. Manuscript in review.
  • Analyzed ctDNA samples of breast cancer patients using Variant Calling methods to detect driver genes and investigate druggable mutations.
  • Investigated the statistical properties of ctDNA data for designing a ctDNA read Simulator.

Pardis Gene Technology Co. | Dec 2018 - June 2019

Bioinformatics R&D Group

  • Analyzed donor-derived cfDNA data of solid organ transplant patients for monitoring graft status and earlier detection of acute rejection episodes.
  • Designed an individual identification SNP panel for the Iranian population based on the Iranome database.

EMBL Heidelberg | June 2017 - Sep 2017

Structural and Computational Biology Unit - Supervisor: Dr. Janosch Hennig

  • Investigated the 3D structure of the TRIM25 protein using integrated structural biology techniques (NMR, X-ray crystallography) along with wet lab procedures for protein expression.

University of Tehran | Mar 2017 - July 2018

Biotechnology Department - Supervisor: Dr. Sayed Amir Marashi

  • Investigated the relation between E. coli metabolic gene co-expression and metabolic flux correlation using microarray gene expression data and E. coli’s metabolic network model sampling results.
  • Applied a 3-way gene expression analysis method called Liquid association on E. coli microarray data to explore the dynamic nature of gene co-expression.
